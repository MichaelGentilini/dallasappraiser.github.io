Just a few fun facts about dogs:

  1. They'll greet you like you've been gone a century when you only went out for a five-minute trip to the store.

  3. Dogs will watch TV with you without hogging the remote.
  4. Dogs find the greatest joy in even the simplest of things, like a ride in the car.
  5. They know how to take awesome selfies.
